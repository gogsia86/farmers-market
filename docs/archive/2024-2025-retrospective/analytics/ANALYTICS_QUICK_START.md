## ğŸš¨ Troubleshooting

### "No data available"
**Cause**: No orders in selected period
**Solution**: 
- Choose longer time period
- Verify farm has products listed
- Check farm status is "APPROVED"

### "Authentication required"
**Cause**: Session expired
**Solution**:
- Log in again
- Check you're using correct account

### "Access denied"
**Cause**: Incorrect role
**Solution**:
- Verify you're logged in as FARMER/ADMIN
- Contact support if role is wrong

### Dashboard loads slowly
**Cause**: Large date range or slow connection
**Solution**:
- Use shorter time periods (7-30 days)
- Check internet connection
- Try again during off-peak hours
